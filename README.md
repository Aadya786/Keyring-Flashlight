 **Keyring-Flashlight**
 - A mini flashlight using AG3 cells, a split ring, an LED light, a PCB, a button, AG3 battery retainers, and heat shrink.
Assembly Procedure
1. Mount AG3 battery retainer into the PCB, and position so extruding metal tab is facing outward
2. Solder the battery retainers to the PCB
3. Shorten the LED and clip them right above the square shape, then bend them in
4. Position the LED's flat side of the LED on the top-side of the PCB
5. Solder the LED to the PCB
6. Mount the push-button to the PCB, solder
7. Install the AG3 cells under the battery retainers, make sure the + terminal of the cells is facing up on the PCB
8. Bend the metal tabs to secure the cells
9. Verify flashlight operation
10. Mount the split ring into the hole at the end of the PCB
11. Slip the heat shrink over the entire thing and heat it up till completely secure and shrunk.
12. Use an X-ACTO knife to cut an opening over the button
